I am Abhinav.V.Jithin of 9th Grade from India. WebsiteX is my project for the project1 of CS50's Web Development with Python and JavaScript. You'll find that this site is related to books as this site is a book review site. To get the complete access to this site, ensure that you have signed in. The features of this site are:
  • Top books on Good Reads will be displayed as soon as you enter the book store.
  • You can search for ISBN,Title,Author or even year related to your search.
  • Even a part of the ISBN or Title or Author is enough to get the results for your search.
  • You can rate any books of this site.
  • Users can comment on any of the books in the site.
  • On the details page of a particular book, you can find the number of comments, goodreads rating and also the average ratings given by the user.
  • API's are there for each book. So app developer's can use this API values for building their apps.
